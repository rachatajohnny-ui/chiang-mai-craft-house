Common questions

Before you write

Custom orders typically take 3–5 weeks from confirmation of design and deposit payment. We will give you a specific date when you confirm your order, and we keep you updated throughout.

Yes — we ship to most countries. International shipping is calculated at checkout based on dimensions and destination. We use EMS for smaller lamps and freight for floor lamps and cluster pendants. We have shipped to Singapore, Japan, Australia, the UK, and the US.

All our lamps use E27 sockets and are rated for LED bulbs up to 15W. We recommend warm white LED (2700–3000K) for the best effect through the weave. Edison-style filament bulbs also work beautifully and are available in our showroom.

Dust with a soft brush or dry cloth. Avoid direct moisture. Keep away from air conditioning vents — sustained dry air can cause splitting over time. Rattan is a living material and will develop a natural patina as it ages. We include a full care card with every lamp.

Because every lamp is made to order, we do not accept returns for change of mind. If your lamp arrives damaged, we will repair or replace it at no cost. Please photograph any damage on arrival and contact us within 48 hours.
